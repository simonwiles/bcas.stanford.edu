Get Involved with BCAS

You are very warmly encouraged to get involved with BCAS. Please subscribe to the mailing list and join the OrgSync group (see below) to keep in touch with Buddhist activities on campus, and please get in touch if you have comments and suggestions about our events. In particular, if you have ideas for events you'd like to see, and like to be part of, please do let us know â€“ we're always looking for new initiatives!

Mailing List

To subscribe / unsubscribe from the BCAS mailing list:

  • Send an email to: bcas-meditation-subscribe@lists.stanford.edu or bcas-meditation-unsubscribe@lists.stanford.edu.
  • The subject line and body is ignored.
  • Make sure the rest of the message is blank.

Alternatively, you can sign yourself up on the bcas-meditation mailman page.

BCAS @ OrgSync

If you have a Stanford SUNetID, you are welcome to officially join the BCAS group at OrgSync. Joining implies no obligation or commitment, but will indicate your interest in and support for the Buddhist Community at Stanford, and may allow you to keep up with BCAS events and activities more easily. The link to the sign-up page is here:
https://orgsync.com/join/44715/buddhist-community-at-stanford
