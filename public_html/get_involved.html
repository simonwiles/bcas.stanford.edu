Mailing List

To subscribe / unsubscribe from the BCAS mailing list:

  • Send an email to: bcas-meditation-subscribe@lists.stanford.edu or bcas-meditation-unsubscribe@lists.stanford.edu.
  • The subject line and body is ignored.
  • Make sure the rest of the message is blank.

Alternatively, you can sign yourself up on the bcas-meditation mailman page.
